ABOUT ANGEL HEART MISSION SCHOOL

Angel Heart Mission School is an independent, coeducational and private institute for 400+ student from Class- Nursery to V.
Angel Heart Mission School is day school for pre-primary and primary students. Our Institute is located in Mahuapara Ambikapur Chhattisgarh.

Angel Heart Mission School emphasizes on 5 fundamental subjects: English, Hindi, Science, Social Science and Mathematics..

OUR MISSION

Mission of Angel Heart Mission School is to give opportunities to each and every children in our school to become a successful and helpful citizen for the community and the Nation, and to open their inner talents and have a bright future for themselves because we believe that each and every children have a hidden talent in them.
